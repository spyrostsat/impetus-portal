This page serves as a portal for the developments of NTUA's Urban Water Management and HydroInformatics group which were achieved under the IMPETUS project.

Digital Twins

Digital Twins of Greece's Attica Region physical entities

A Regional Digital Twin (DT) is being implemented in Greece's Attica region as part of the IMPETUS project. It leverages real-time data on climate adaptation initiatives and solutions to serve as a central hub for knowledge and action. The platform facilitates a co-creation process involving stakeholders and policymakers to drive green business development and climate resilience.

HIPS

Hot-spots Identification and Prioritization Service

HIPS, developed under the EU-funded IMPETUS project, enables stakeholders to conduct customized hotspot analyses by combining future climate projections with various indicators. It supports decision-making and regional adaptation strategies using a comprehensive framework of metrics. As the project progresses, HIPS will be enhanced with additional datasets and features.
